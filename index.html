About Us

Security is the key factor of this era. We always work for making the world more safer each day

We are a group of Cyber Security enthusiastic students working together in a peer-to-peer learning platform for making our information more secure day by day. We have joined our hands to create awareness among people for practicing secure methods wherever possible. We will try our best to bring out privacy and security oriented mindset within the young minds.

OUR MISSION AND VISION

We are a group of Cyber Security enthusiastic students working together in a peer-to-peer learning platform for making our information more secure day by day.

We have joined our hands to create awareness among people for practicing secure methods wherever possible.

We will try our best to bring out privacy and security oriented mindset within the young minds.

What Keeps Us Motivated?

Once a wise man said, 'Security is just an illusion. Everything can be compromised given enough time.’
We have taken those words into our heart and the essence of it is flowing through our veins.
